join our community

slashrocket is a community of developers of all backgrounds. we started with a few people talking about code and have since grown to a community of over 500 members. we work together to create projects as a group, discuss development, and provide a friendly home on the web for developers. we adhere to a code of conduct so everyone feels welcome.
